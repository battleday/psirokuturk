Debriefing

Thank you for your participation in our study! Your anonymous data makes an important contribution to our understanding of human learning and memory.

The study you just participated in is part of a series of studies looking at human inference. The goal of this particular study is to see how people use different kinds of information to make similarity judgments.

In this study, you made similarity judgments between stimuli with different groups of shapes. We are interested in how your answers to questions reveal which kinds of information shape those judgments and change over time.

Thank you again for participating in our research!
